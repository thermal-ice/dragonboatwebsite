Learn to Paddle

Learn to Paddle with the UTSC Dragon Boat Team this Summer

UTSC's Dragon Boat team Crimson Tide is hosting "Learn to Paddle" for the month of July! It's a great opportunity to try out a new sport and meet some of the current team members.

Each practice will be held at near the Outer Harbor Marina. We'll have one hour of land practice (form training, fundamentals), as well as one hour of water practice (learning to paddle in an actual dragon boat).

Come learn to paddle at Outer Harbour and bring your friends! Everyone's invited - no need to be an UTSC student, nor even an university student.

Reach us by dropping us a message on facebook or emailing us at utscdboat@gmail.com
